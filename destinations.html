Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit

Spring (March-May) and Fall (September-November) offer ideal weather across Morocco.

Currency & Money

Moroccan Dirham (MAD). ATMs widely available. Cash preferred in markets and small shops.

Language

Arabic and Berber are official. French widely spoken. English in tourist areas.

Safety

Morocco is generally safe. Stay alert in crowded areas and use registered guides.
