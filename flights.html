Flight Booking Tips

01

Book in Advance

Book 2-3 months ahead for the best prices, especially during peak seasons (March-May, September-November).

02

Be Flexible

Flying mid-week (Tuesday-Thursday) is often cheaper. Consider nearby airports for better deals.

03

Compare Prices

Check multiple booking sites and airlines. Direct flights cost more but save time.

04

Visa Requirements

Many nationalities get 90-day visa-free entry. Check requirements before booking.
